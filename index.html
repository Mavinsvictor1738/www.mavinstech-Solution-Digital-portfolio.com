About Me

Victor Chitole

Founder & CEO, Mavinstech Solution

I am a passionate frontend developer and technology educator based in Kilifi, Kenya. With extensive experience in web development and digital education, I specialize in creating accessible, user-friendly digital experiences that bridge the technology gap in underserved communities.

Through Mavinstech Solution, the digital agency I founded, we provide comprehensive technology solutions including web design, educational technology training, graphics design, and ICT consultancy. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with the digital skills needed to thrive in today's technology-driven world.

My work with the Kolibri platform has been particularly impactful, bringing offline digital learning to schools and community centers across Kilifi County. I'm committed to making technology education accessible to all, regardless of internet connectivity or resources.
